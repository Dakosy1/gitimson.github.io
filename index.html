Welcome to my blog! I'm a 17-year-old Character Artist who loves to share my passion for Game Art with the world. This blog serves as a platform for me to showcase my artwork and share my creative journey with others. In addition to my blog posts, which I like to call recipes, I also offer character art services through a separate page where you can browse my portfolio and request custom character designs. I'm always excited to collaborate with clients and bring their unique characters to life. While my focus is on sharing my artistic journey and passion with others, I also feature products on occasion, such as prints of my artwork, 3D models and in-depth courses. Thank you for visiting my blog and supporting my creative endeavors. I hope that my art inspires you and brings a little bit of joy to your day.
